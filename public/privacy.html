Privacy

Watchlist is a personal vintage-watch tool built by one person as a passion project. Below is everything that gets stored when you use it.

What you give us when you sign in

Google sign-in only. We receive your email and the name on your Google profile. No password, no phone number, no address.

What you create while you use the site

Hearts, hidden items, lists you build, watches you mark as owned or sold, your shortlist, journal notes you write on a watch, reactions on shared lists, saved searches, the display name you pick in Settings, your preferred currency. Each of these is stored against your account in Supabase with row-level security — only you (and people you've invited as collaborators on a shared list) can read your rows.

Photos you upload

Photos for a manually-entered watch live in a private Supabase Storage bucket scoped to your user. We resize them client-side before upload to keep the file small.

Cached dealer images

Dealer images for the watches you've hearted are cached in Vercel Blob so they keep working even after a dealer takes the listing down. We don't cache the whole feed — only items you save.

Anonymous usage data

Watchlist records six event types — view, click, save, hide, add-to-list, share — keyed to a random UUID stored in your browser's localStorage. There is no IP address, no device fingerprint, no third-party analytics product. Raw events are pruned after 90 days; aggregated rollups are kept to understand which dealers and brands people engage with. Sign-in status doesn't change what's recorded; the same UUID is used either way.

What we never store

Payment info, addresses, phone numbers, browsing on other sites, sale or transaction details. Watchlist is a directory layer that links to dealers — we don't broker sales and don't see them.

Who else sees your data

Supabase hosts the database, auth, and your uploaded photos. Vercel hosts the site and the dealer-image cache. Google handles sign-in and sees the OAuth event. When you follow a link to a dealer's site, that dealer's server sees a referer header indicating you came from the-watch-list.app. No analytics products, no ad networks, no data sales.

Cookies and local storage

Watchlist uses your browser's localStorage for view preferences (theme, columns, last-active sub-tabs), the anonymous telemetry UUID, and a Supabase auth token while you're signed in. There are no third-party tracking cookies.

Sharing lists with others

A list you share via the Collaboration Link is visible to people you've invited by email and accepted the invite. The View Only Link creates a "save a copy" experience for the recipient — the copy lives in their account and is theirs from then on.

Deleting your account

Email hello@the-watch-list.app and I'll delete every row and uploaded photo connected to your account. There isn't a self-serve delete button yet.

Changes
